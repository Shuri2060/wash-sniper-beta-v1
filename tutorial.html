You may use separate wallets for holding $WASH and sniping.

  1. Select wallet (EVM address) holding $WASH in your wallet extension (Rabby, Metamask, etc). Sign tx to prove ownership & login.
  2. Select sniper wallet in your wallet extension. Fund with Hyperliquid L1 spot USDC to snipe. Enable the sniper (similar process to Establishing Connection on HL - this creates an API wallet)
  3. Queue your sniping orders. Your sniping Marketcap is determined by your price, it will show. Note you are choosing the worst price you can accept, it could fill for better.
  4. Trigger the sniper. This can be done manually or by setting the timer. The sniping page needs to stay open, and ideally focused. This is a local sniper
  5. To check how the sniper did, check your trade history on the sniping wallet on the Hyperliquid Frontend. (TODO: will add this on page directly later)
    • Order History: This is your sniping attempts. If you see none there, the sniper failed to work (report this)
    • Trade History: This is your fills
